Breadcrumbs

That little trail of elements with the arrows above is your breadcrumbs component. It follows similar rules to that of the sidenav's data (since they're both compiled in the same algorithm).

You can find the component inside of the mdx_layout.jsx file.

Unlike the sidenav and header you cannot control the order of the breadcrumb list

Title (String)

Typical Title Rule shenanigans for label purposes. Learn more about it here

---
title: "Label that shows up on Sidenav, Header, and Breadcrumbs"
---

hideBreadcrumbs (Boolean)

True/False boolean for whether or not to show the breadcrumbs component.

---
hideBreadcrumbs: false
---
